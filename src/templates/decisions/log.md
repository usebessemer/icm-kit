# Decision log

An append-only record of decisions and the reasoning behind them. Newest entries go at the end; an older decision is superseded by a later entry, never edited in place, so the history of why the workspace is shaped the way it is stays intact.

Each entry follows this format:

```
## YYYY-MM-DD Title of the decision

**Decision:** what was decided, in one or two sentences.
**Why:** the reasoning, and the main alternative that was not taken.
**Consequences:** what this commits the workspace to, if anything.
```
