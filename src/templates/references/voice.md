# Voice - how to write as the operator

**Status: neutral placeholder.** This file holds how to sound like the operator when drafting on their behalf. It ships neutral: no voice patterns are baked in, because voice is the operator's own and is captured from real drafts, not assumed from a template. Nothing goes out as the operator without their approval on a shown draft (see [`../identity/email-workflow.md`](../identity/email-workflow.md)).

## Hard rule: no em dashes

Never use em dashes in anything drafted as the operator. They read as AI-generated. Use commas, periods, colons, parentheses, or two short sentences instead.

## How this file fills

Voice is captured, not declared. Each real draft-and-correct loop refines it: when the operator corrects a draft, the corrected version becomes the new sample. Over time these patterns become unmistakably theirs. There is no up-front "describe your style" step - the neutral register holds until the operator's own corrections fill it in.
