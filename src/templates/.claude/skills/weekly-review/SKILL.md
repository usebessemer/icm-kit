# Weekly review

A recurring pass to keep the workspace healthy and the operator informed. Run it once a week, or on demand when things feel out of date.

## Steps

1. Run the structure audit from the workspace root with `icm-kit audit` and note any findings. A clean run is the baseline; anything flagged is the first thing to raise.
2. Read the state board and reconcile it with reality, moving finished items to the done tail and flagging anything that has stalled.
3. Skim the channels and the decision log for the week, and pull together the handful of things the operator actually needs to see.

## Tone

Keep the summary short and honest. Lead with what needs a decision, follow with what changed, and leave out the routine. The operator should be able to read it in a minute and know exactly what, if anything, is being asked of them.

## What good looks like

A review is doing its job when nothing important is a surprise: the board matches reality, the audit is clean or its findings are named, and every open decision is in front of the operator rather than waiting silently.
